Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

At StitchLab, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy documents how we collect, protect, and use your information when you access and use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We only access or request information that is necessary to facilitate your training, achievements, and cloud synchronization:

  • Authentication Information: If you register or log in using Google Authentication or your Email, we receive basic identifiers (e.g., your name, email address, and profile picture).
  • Application Data & Progress: Your points, game progression, learning stats, customized flashcards, and preferences.
  • Google Drive Storage Access: When you connect your Google Drive, we only access files within your Drive that are specifically created or read by StitchLab for backup purposes (utilizing drive.file and drive.appdata scopes). We do not read or modify your other personal files.

2. How We Use Informational Assets

Your data is used entirely to support your learning experience:

  • To secure and sync your customized flashcards, stats, and streaks seamlessly.
  • To manage backup configurations and restore your progress on other devices.
  • To customize interactive lessons and adjust training levels.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your learning progress backups on Google Drive are securely transmitted and written directly to your personal Google Cloud Storage spaces with 100% encryption.

4. Third-Party Sharing

StitchLab does not sell, trade, or transfer your personal identification credentials or file data to third-party entities. All credentials remain secure within the Firebase & Google Authorization pipelines.

5. Your Choices and Consent

By using our service, you consent to this Privacy Policy. You can disconnect Google Drive synchronization or delete your data at any time from the settings panel inside StitchLab.
